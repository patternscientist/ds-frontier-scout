# Skeptical Audit

- Why might this not actually be open? Later history-independent file-system or allocator work may solve it.
- Why might it be too saturated? It is not saturated; the risk is that the exact model is obsolete.
- Why might automation fail? Distributional equality over layouts gets large quickly.
- What would falsify interest? A later low-overhead variable-size allocator with strong history independence.
- Primary sources to check next: post-2001 citations of Naor-Teague and practical history-independent storage papers.
