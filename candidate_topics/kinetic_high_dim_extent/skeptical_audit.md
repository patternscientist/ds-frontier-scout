# Skeptical Audit

- Why might this not actually be open? Later kinetic geometry work may solve specific 3D extent cases.
- Why might it be too saturated? Kinetic data structures are niche, not saturated, but the classic problems are old.
- Why might automation fail? Algebraic event complexity can hide in large constructed instances.
- What would falsify interest? A modern survey giving tight higher-dimensional KDS bounds for the chosen measure.
- Primary sources to check next: Basch-Guibas-Hershberger, Agarwal-Guibas-Hershberger-Veach, and post-2016 kinetic proximity papers.
