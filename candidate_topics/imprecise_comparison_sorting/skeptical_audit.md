# Skeptical Audit

- Why might this not actually be open? Noisy/imprecise sorting has follow-up literature not fully checked.
- Why might it be too saturated? It is not a flagship data-structure problem; saturation risk is moderate-low.
- Why might automation fail? Small randomized strategies may not generalize.
- What would falsify interest? A later `O(n)` randomized error-2 algorithm or matching lower bound.
- Primary sources to check next: citations of arXiv:1501.02911 and noisy tournament algorithms after 2015.
