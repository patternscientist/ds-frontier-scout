# Skeptical Audit

- Why might this not actually be open? Later range-mode papers may have stronger conditional lower bounds or better upper bounds than this pass checked.
- Why might it be too saturated? Range queries are a mature area, but exact range mode is narrower than the headline structures.
- Why might automation fail? Finite arrays may not reveal asymptotic lower-bound structure.
- What would falsify interest? A modern paper closing the linear-space gap to polylogarithmic or proving a strong conditional barrier.
- Primary sources to check next: the ToCS version of Chan et al., Greve-Jorgensen-Larsen-Truelsen ICALP 2010, and post-2018 dynamic/conditional range-mode papers.
