# Skeptical Audit

- Why might this not actually be open? The exact `O(log log m)` worst-case linear-space result may exist in a later thesis/paper not checked here.
- Why might it be too saturated? It is old, but not obviously saturated; the bigger risk is obscurity.
- Why might automation fail? The challenge is a word-RAM layout proof, not just finding good small version trees.
- What would falsify interest? A direct construction with linear space and worst-case optimal time, or a matching lower bound.
- Primary sources to check next: Straka thesis Section on persistent arrays, Dietz WADS 1989, and any post-2013 persistent-array citations.
