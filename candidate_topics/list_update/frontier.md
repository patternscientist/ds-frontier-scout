# Frontier

Verified source:

- Christoph Ambuehl, Bernd Gaertner, and Bernhard von Stengel, "Optimal Lower Bounds for Projective List Update Algorithms": https://arxiv.org/abs/1002.2440

The source states that the optimal randomized competitive ratio is open between 1.5 and 1.6, and proves that projective algorithms cannot beat 1.6 in the partial-cost model.
